How to create proposed elevations

Example elevation

Your elevations must

  • Be drawn to scale Usually 1:100 or 1:50 at A3 or A4 size
  • Have a scale bar
  • Have a unique drawing reference number
  • Be clearly labelled
  • Show all sides of the proposal

You should include

  • Materials labelling any materials that are relevant for the proposed changes
  • Heights showing any heights relative to the street if on a hill

Your application may not be marked as valid if the drawings do not clearly and accurately describe the site and the proposed changes.
