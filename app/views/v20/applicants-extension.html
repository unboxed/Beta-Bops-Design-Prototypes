Ask for a time extension

Send a message to the case officer to let them know you need more time to respond to the requests.

What you need to do:

  • Explain how long you need
  • List each request which needs a time extension

What happens next:

  • The officer will be sent the request and may contact you if they need any further detail
  • If you do not hear from the officer you can assume your time extension has been accepted
  • Any requests which show as overdue will not automatically close your case
