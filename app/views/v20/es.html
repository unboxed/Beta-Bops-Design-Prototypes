How to create existing sections

Example section

Your sections must

  • Be drawn to scale Usually 1:100 or 1:50 at A3 or A4 size
  • Have a scale bar
  • Have a unique drawing reference number

You should include

  • An off-site datum point as a point of reference for levels
  • Floor to ceiling heights within the building
  • Eaves, foundations and retaining structures if they might encroach onto adjoining land
  • Voids if there are any within the building

Your application may not be marked as valid if the drawings do not clearly and accurately describe the site and the proposed changes.
